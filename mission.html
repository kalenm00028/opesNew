Opes
OPES CONSILIUM PARIT
Consilium

THE THREE TENETS OF OPES CONSILIUM:

  • Knowledge is a choice - choose wisely.
  • An open mind is a healthy mind - opinions are meant to be debated.
  • Value all forms of knowledge - education leads to understanding, which complements innovation, which begets progress.

PURPOSE:

  • To facilitate innovation through interdisciplinary communication.

VISION:

  • Opes will be a global network of human resources, comprised of experts from every field, and nearly every country.
  • Through a web platform, offer an expansive network of human resources to OPES members.
  • Through our server, offer computer programs to assist in projects involving statistics, 3D modeling, and more.
  • Host Ted-style talks
  • Operate scientific laboratories for research and development
